# field.hockey Field Hockey

Field Hockey

Field Hockey is a game played on a field that looks like a football field. There is a net at each end, in which two teams of 11 players each compete in driving a small ball into the other's goal. Each player has a stick with a curved end that is flat on one side and rounded on the other

  • 11 players on the field
  • 3-5 players in each position
  • Played at a High School and College level
  • players have to wear specific gear
  • Goggles and shinguards protect the player
  • A mouthguard protects from concussions
